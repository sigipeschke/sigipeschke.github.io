A collection of my assignments from computational physics 381, 481, and 581 courses at the University of Calgary which emphasized exploration of tensorflow, ordinary differential equations, statistical analysis, polynomials, and various niche topics.

•Exploring the effect of image preprocessing on TensorFlow image classification •Parralelizing computation in TensorFlow •TensorFlow introduction by determining slope and intercept from data points •Simplifying data utilizing principal component analysis and singular spectrum analysis •Evaluation of the error, complementary error, and Faddeeva functions •Solving 1-D Diffusion and Schrödinger equations using Euler and Runge-Kutta method •Solving Poisson's equation using the finite-difference approximation •Fractals and the Mandlebrot set •Exploring the Ising model of magnetic dipole moments and spin states •Statistical analysis of the system energy of the Pauli Paramagnet •Analyzing the relationship between spin orientations, magnetization, temperature, total energy and entropy •Analysis of damped driven harmonic oscillators by solving ordinary differential equations •Statistical analysis of random interacting walkers on a 1-D lattice •Exploring steady states of a 1-D random walk utilizing Markov chain •Analysis of pseudo random number generators and cellular automata •Statistical analysis of die rolls and Lossless Image Compression •Trend Fitting and extrapolating Carbon Dioxide concentration data •Chebyshev Polynomials and Lissajous Figures •Legendre Polynomials •Polynomial Evaluation and Taylor series •Computational Integration •Riffle shuffle, Arnold's map, Order Finding and Integer Factorization
